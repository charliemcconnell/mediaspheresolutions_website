Where It All Started

MediaSphere Solutions was founded on a simple observation: most service businesses are great at what they do, but lose time and money because their marketing, bookings, follow-ups, and admin all live in separate places, or don't exist at all.

We set out to change that. By combining smart marketing, AI-powered automation, and a connected CRM into one cohesive system, we give service businesses the same kind of infrastructure that big companies rely on, without the complexity or the price tag.

Today we work with businesses across Ireland and beyond, helping them get found online, convert more enquiries, and stop letting leads fall through the cracks.
